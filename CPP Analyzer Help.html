Welcome to CPP Analyzer


CPP Analyzer is designed to help financial planners and advisors navigate the complex process of helping their clients decide when is the best

age (60 to 70) to begin their CPP benefit.  CPP Analyzer allows the planner to look at the details of the CPP benefit, Post Retirement Benefit, and

the Survivor benefit and then integrate this information with the rest of their Retirement Income plan. The planner of course should consider all aspects

of a client's situation before making a recommendation.


•The software has been designed to assist with clients 55 years of age and older.


•During the client data input stage the software helps guide the planner through 7 clearly defined steps making the software easy to use.


•During the analysis stage it allows the planner to compare in real time (with the client if they like) two client scenarios in order to demonstrate the effects of:


▪Different starting ages for the the CPP benefit,

▪Different life expectancy, and

▪Different future CPP benefit inflation, wage growth, and discount rates.



•For the very complex analysis of a client's Survivor Benefit, CPP Analyzer allows the planner to look at the CPP survivor benefit (Combined Survivor benefit) and how

it integrates with the regular CPP benefit.


•CPP Analyzer also allows the planner to tailor the client report from very simple to more complex reports to further reinforce the effects of timing when considering  when

to begin their CPP benefit.
