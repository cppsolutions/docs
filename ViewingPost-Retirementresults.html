Viewing Post-Retirement results

To navigate to the PRB analysis either use:

•the Ribbon Tabs > View Tab > Click the "PRB analysis" button. or

•the Configuration panel > Click the "PRB analysis" button.


To Exit the form Click the  button (Bottom right corner)



if your client continues to work after the age of 65, the Over 65 - Post Retirement Benefit analysis will aid them in making the decision as to weather

they should continue to contribution to CPP (adding to the Post Retirement Benefit).


The report will show results for the scenario if it has been chosen to model the CPP + Post Retirement option (Client Scenarios) and

the client has chosen to continue work beyond the age of 65.




Age

•Age of client when contributions are made

Year

•Year when contributions are made

Contribution

•The total amount of contributions mage that year

Benefit

•The increase in PRB  benefit do to the contributions from the previous year

Lifetime benefit

•The lifetime benefit of the contribution for that year, the Total Lifetime benefit is indicated in the Totals row

Break even

•The break even point of when the amount of contribution = total benefit received (constant dollar comparison)
